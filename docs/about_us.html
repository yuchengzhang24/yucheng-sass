About Us

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is the lead vocalist of the band. A native Austinite, he has been singing, songwriting, and playing the guitar since the age of 8, and is a fan-favorite throughout local music venues.

Bruce Johnson

Bruce Johnson is the lead guitarist of the band. He has been playing the electric and acoustic guitar and bass ever since he can remember. Born in Nashville, he moved to Austin after catching the eye of Harvey Dale as an opening act during Austin City Limits.

Sam Anderson

Sam Anderson is the drummer and lead songwriter for the band. Born in Louisiana, he moved to Austin to enroll at the Butler School of Music at the University of Texas at Austin.
